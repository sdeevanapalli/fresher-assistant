Campus Navigation

SAC

The Student Activities Centre (SAC) is bustling with activity on its two floors. The lower floor offers recreational spaces like pool and billiards tables, a punching bag, dance room, and sports courts. The gym caters to fitness needs, while the upper floor hosts club meetings and rehearsals in various dedicated rooms. Tucked under a staircase is the Tinkerer’s Lab, a haven for innovators. And while the first-floor balcony offers a serene view, the SAC Basement remains a mystery.

Mess 1

Mess 1 is the hotspot on campus, with reservations disappearing in seconds. It's where groups gather, doubling table capacities and strengthening friendships. Aditya Caterers runs the show, occasionally spicing things up with special menus for a price. When the regular menu gets old, Yummpy’s and other stalls outside offer relief. And during exams, Mess 1 becomes the All Night Canteen (ANC), serving up comfort food for late-night study sessions..

Mess 2

Across the old hockey ground sits Mess 2, another popular spot managed by Shakthi Caterers. It's where friendships are forged over meals, despite occasional separations from friends. Special menus are also on offer here, albeit with an extra cost. When cravings strike, Yummpy’s and neighboring stalls provide alternatives. And during exams, Mess 2 transforms into the All Night Canteen (ANC), fueling students' late-night study sessions.

Library

The campus library is a sanctuary for study and leisure, offering diverse resources and tranquil study spaces. From textbooks to novels, it caters to every intellectual pursuit. Librarians stand ready to assist with research inquiries or recommend the perfect read. It's equipped with computers, printers, and ample resources for scholarly pursuits, fostering a culture of learning and discovery on campus..

Auditorium

The campus auditorium is a dynamic space for lectures, performances, and events, featuring modern facilities and ample seating. Managed by dedicated staff, it hosts a variety of cultural and academic activities, enriching campus life and fostering community engagement..

Connaught Place

Connaught Place, affectionately known as CP, stands as the bustling hub of activity at BITS Pilani. This campus cornerstone offers a range of essential amenities catering to the diverse needs of students. From a bookstore and stationery shop for academic supplies to a hair salon and gift shops for personal needs, CP ensures students have access to everything they require. Additionally, it hosts facilities like a bank branch, medical shop, laundry service, and general store, further enhancing its utility. Moreover, CP serves as a convenient delivery point for couriers from online platforms, streamlining the process for students. With its central location and comprehensive offerings, CP plays a vital role in facilitating the daily routines of BITSians.

Fountain

The fountain, gracefully adorning the entrance of the main gate, serves as a welcoming symbol, its cascading waters evoking a sense of serenity and grandeur. As students pass by, it stands as a reminder of the institution's legacy and commitment to beauty amidst academia. Its tranquil presence invites contemplation and reflection, offering a moment of respite from the bustling energy of campus life. With each ripple and splash, the fountain embodies the spirit of renewal and growth, inspiring all who encounter it on their journey through the gates of learning.

Lover's Lane

LL, or lovers lane, ironically lives up to its name by being isolated, contrary to expectations.

Road Not Taken

RNT, the road not taken, serves as a stark reminder of singleness, deterring those uninterested in such reflections.

Swimming Pool

A recently opened facility, the swimming pool is a recreational area with a pool that is 25m long and 1.5m deep. Timings are strict and so is the swimwear allowed. Not recommended for those with seasonal allergies or Cherophobia.

Rock Garden

The rock garden, a scenic spot on campus, captivates with its natural beauty. Amidst lush greenery and carefully arranged rocks, it offers a tranquil escape for contemplation or leisurely walks. It's a peaceful haven where students and faculty can find solace amidst the busyness of academic life.

Director's Quarter

The director's quarter, nestled in the campus center, stands as a symbol of exclusivity amidst the student hubbub. Surrounded by activity, its closed gates hint at the privilege reserved for a select few. Despite its central location, it remains a silent observer of the campus dynamics, a nucleus of power and influence.

Medical Centre

In the realm of exams, the medical centre becomes our ultimate rescue center! Whenever you find yourself unable to take an exam to claim this makeup chance, simply show your medical centre receipt as proof of your need for a rescheduled exam.
